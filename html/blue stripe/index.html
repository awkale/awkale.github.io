ABOUT

Blue Stripe PR is a full-service, fashion, and accessories public relations firm. Realizing the designer contemporary market lacked the attention it deserved, Blue Stripe PR was created to provide a luxury level of client service and press work to a saturated and largely unrecognized market. Years of focus and experience working with emerging designers to craft and enact public relations campaigns have made our showroom a reliable resource for editors and stylists to discover and support emerging and established brands.

Located in Chelsea, the showroom is housed in a large, modern space that showcases a mix of fashion and accessories collections. Our capabilities are vast and cover a broad spectrum, including creating client awareness amongst influential editors, stylists, publicists and celebrities, securing editorial features, product placement, developing and enacting creative initiatives, aligning clients with strategic partnerships, and increasing overall brand recognition. In addition we have an online media team that concentrates on social media relations, online strategy, and blog development. Expanding social networking and blogger outreach is a specialty.

BLUE STRIPE PR/BPCM AFFILIATION

The relationship between Blue Stripe and BPCM is one of a unique nature. Blue Stripe PR works under the same principals as sister agency BPCM while maintaining the intimacy of a boutique agency. Clients of Blue Stripe are fortunate in that they have access to the boundless resources of a larger agency like BPCM. SERVICES

Successful companies count public relations as an integral part of any effective business plan. We strive to give our clients a clear analysis of their place in the market and a strategic public relations plan that highlights the best way forward. Our personalized approach, combining traditional and digital strategies, to every client allows us to garner exceptional results for brands.

Our team of experienced professionals has strong relationships with journalists, editors, stylists, celebrities, bloggers and media across all fields. Our work is strategy-driven public relations with goals and benchmarks set so that all parties know what to expect. When working with a new client we assign teams that consist of both senior and junior staff. Partners in the agency are always available for meetings and fully involved in the development of PR strategies for our clients.
